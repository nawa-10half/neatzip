NeatZip is a single-purpose utility that creates "clean" ZIP archives — ZIPs that do not contain macOS-only metadata such as __MACOSX, .DS_Store, or ._* files.

How to test:
1) Drag any file or folder onto the NeatZip window, OR
2) In Finder, Control-click (right-click) a file or folder and choose Services > "Clean ZIP with NeatZip…".

A small panel then lets you optionally set a password (ZipCrypto for compatibility, or AES-256 for strong encryption). Because the app is sandboxed, the resulting .zip is written through the standard save panel (it defaults to the same folder as the source).

No account, login, or in-app purchase is required to use the app. NeatZip collects no data and works fully offline.
